Shunsuke Michieda (Japanese: 道枝 駿佑, romanized: Michieda Shunsuke, born 25 July 2002, in Osaka Prefecture) is a Japanese actor, singer and talent who is a member of Japanese idol group Naniwa Danshi under Johnny & Associates.

Biography
His mother is a big fan of SMAP, he grew up listening to songs from SMAP and Hey! Say! JUMP from a very young age, and he also like Johnny's. While looking at the drama of 2014 broadcast of Kindaichi Shōnen no Jikenbo N starring Ryosuke Yamada of Hey! Say! JUMP and longing to send a resume to Johnny & Associates.
